About Edu Hub

EduHub is an open platform that promotes & help young researchers, students and academicians (primarily from Law universities) to co-create, share and publish their work in open. This will foster more the culture of learning by collaboration and sharing to help advance research capacity and data on diverse subjects in the country.

Eduhub’s goal is to help young researchers get more comfortable with the principles of open research, open publishing, co-creating open data and develop analytical capabilities, to help advance research and data ecosystem in India.

EduHub can be self-hosted in university’s digital infrastructure or can be requested as SaaS application at nominal hosting pricing. For more details, please drop us a note at eduhub@civicdatalab.in.

Background

Justice Hub was born out of the Agami Data for Justice Challenge in June 2019. The Challenge sought to curate a collaborative entrepreneurial effort that increases and evolves the use of data. Inputs from a wide range of changemakers, led us to see the opportunity to collaborate on each other’s datasets.

The Challenge invited applicants to create a data hub and create new datasets, and after a rigorous selection process CivicDataLab was selected as the winner to create the Justice Hub.
